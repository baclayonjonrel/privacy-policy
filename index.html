Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Lumen Bible: Daily Light ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may access, store, and use.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number through the App.

The App may store certain data locally on your device to support features such as:

  • selected Bible translation and language
  • bookmarks, highlights, and reading/listening progress
  • saved audio, preferences, and widget settings
  • daily verse and home screen widget content

2. Device and Technical Data

Depending on your device and platform, limited technical information may be processed automatically, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and basic diagnostic information needed for stability and compatibility.

3. Internet Access

The App may use an internet connection to load Bible content, download app-related assets, support audio features, and keep content functioning properly. Some content may also be available offline after it has been downloaded or stored on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

If third-party platform or infrastructure services are used by your device or app store provider, those services may process limited technical data under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be deleted by your device unless your platform preserves certain app data or backups.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce privacy and security risks, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the updated version of this page.
